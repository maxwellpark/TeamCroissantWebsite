Our Work

Starlight Express

Welcome to Starlight Express. Please ignore the impending collapse of reality as you know it, your interplanetary shipping company isn't going to run itself!

Starlight Express is a story driven, sci-fi life sim inspired by Stardew Valley and Mass Effect 2.

Candy Core Crisis

Candy Core Crisis is a flight combat simulator with light tower defense/RTS elements! Play as a ragtag, candy themed mercenary group tasked with defending the natural resources of Lollipop Lake!

Princesa do Coração

"A Princesa do Coração" ("The Heart Princess" in English) is a deeply personal visual novel that ambitiously blends delicate pixel art with traditional non-digital paintings. Team Croissant pixel artist and illustrator Bárbara Victoria chose to make a game for her undergraduate thesis and the team was more than happy to help!
